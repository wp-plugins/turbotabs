A) Introduction

TurboTabs is a responsive tabs WordPress plugin designed and coded with intention to extract to the front-end user all the possibilities and power to customize and edit tabbed content as they please without any codding knowledge. Everything is done via few clicks, detailed instructions and live preview are also featured on the editing page. TurboTabs comes in 4 Layouts - TurboTab, Classic, Simple and Hollow and each layout has it's Vertical, Horizontal and Accordion mode. Every mode and layout is fully customizable, so to say, possibilities of styling are UNLIMITED .

On smaller screens and devices tab is transformed into accordion. You can insert any content into TurboTab, be it text, image, video or [shortcode]. It features resposive images, and videos - which means any video or image you put into TurboTab will automatically become responsive. It also comes with 28 Animations and options like navigation tabs alignment and positioning. Tabs can be positioned to Top or Bottom for horizontal mode and to Left or Right for vertical mode. According to that tabs can be further aligned to Left, Centered or pushed to Right in horizontal mode and aligned to Top, Middle, or Bottom in vertical mode.

Every layout is illustrated with pictures and explained in detail - describing you what are it's advantages and what can be applied to that specific layout, and what cannot.
