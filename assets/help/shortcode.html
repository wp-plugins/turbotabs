Shortcode

Inserting a shortcode is done in two clicks: 1. Click the TurboTabs icon from the tinyMCE editor and 2. Select your group from the drop-down menu of currently available groups.

  • 1.

  • 2.

To use a shortcode manualy just insert [turbotabs group="group_slug"] where group_slug is your selected group's slug. "Slug" is url-friendly name that is assigned to every group when creating it. If you didn't edited it manualy, then group "slug" will be the name of your group just lowercased. To find your group slug, go to admin dashboard, click Groups and then edit choosen group. On edit page you will see three fields: Name, Slug And Customization Options
